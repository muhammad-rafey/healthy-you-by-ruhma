We work hard to make every guidebook and program worth your time. We also know that sometimes a purchase doesn't fit, and you may need to ask for a refund. This page explains when that's possible.

## Eligibility

Refund requests must be made within thirty days of the original purchase date. Requests should be sent to <a href="mailto:info@dietitianruhma.com">info@dietitianruhma.com</a> with your order number and a short note about why you are requesting the refund.

For guidebooks bought through an external store (Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Amazon KDP, or similar) please use that store's refund process — we don't have the technical access to reverse those charges directly.

## Non-refundable items

Because guidebooks are digital and downloaded immediately, all purchases are considered final and non-refundable once the download link has been accessed or the file has been opened. We don't offer refunds for change of mind or for general dissatisfaction with the content.

## Programs

Coaching and Diet Planning are different. If after forty days of working together you are not seeing the changes we set out in your initial assessment, you can stop the program and we will return fifty per cent of the fee — no questions asked.

If you need to end a program early for personal reasons we'll always part ways amicably and prorate any unused time on a case-by-case basis.

## Process

Once a refund is approved we send the funds back to the payment method used for the original purchase. Please allow five to seven business days for the refund to settle in your account.

## Exceptions

Refunds may be granted outside the normal rules in exceptional cases — for example duplicate purchases made in error, technical issues that prevent access to a guidebook, or significant inaccuracies in published material. Get in touch and we'll work it out.

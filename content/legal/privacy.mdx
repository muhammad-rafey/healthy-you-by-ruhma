This Privacy Policy describes how dietitianruhma.com collects, uses, and shares information when you visit the site or make a purchase.

## Information we collect

**Device information.** When you visit the site we automatically collect technical details about the device you use — your browser, IP address, time zone, and the cookies installed on your device. As you browse we record which pages you view, how you arrived at the site, and how you interact with it. We refer to this information as "Device Information."

**Order information.** When you buy a guidebook through one of the external stores linked from the Library, the relevant store collects your name, billing details, payment information, email, and phone number. We refer to anything we receive about a purchase as "Order Information."

## How we use your information

We use Order Information to fulfil and support purchases — sending receipts, providing access to digital downloads, and answering follow-up questions. With your permission we may also send occasional updates about new programs or guidebooks.

We use Device Information to keep the site secure, screen for fraud, and improve the experience — primarily through aggregate analytics, never to identify individuals beyond what is required to deliver a service.

## Sharing your information

We share information with the third parties that help us operate — for example, the storefront platform you bought a guidebook from, the email service that sends order confirmations, and the analytics provider we use to understand site traffic.

We may also share information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a lawful request, or to otherwise protect our rights.

## Your rights

If you live in a region with data-protection laws (the EU/UK, for example) you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Email <a href="mailto:info@dietitianruhma.com">info@dietitianruhma.com</a> and we will action your request within a reasonable timeframe.

## Data retention

We keep Order Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete it. We keep Device Information for the time required to operate analytics — typically twelve months.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "last updated" date at the top of this page is always current.

## Contact

For any questions about our privacy practices, write to <a href="mailto:info@dietitianruhma.com">info@dietitianruhma.com</a>.

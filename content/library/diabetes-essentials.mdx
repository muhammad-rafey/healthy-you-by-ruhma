A clear, practical guide to managing diabetes and insulin resistance. Diabetes Essentials is written for the person who wants to understand what's happening in their body and what to do about it — without the panic, the contradictions, or the sales pitches that come with most diabetes content online.

The book covers Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes as well as insulin resistance — the often-overlooked precursor that's quietly affecting a generation of women. It includes practical lifestyle modifications, detailed workout plans tailored to the specific needs of people with diabetes, and meal patterns that focus on the foods most likely to actually help.

You'll find clear insights into which foods support your condition and how they interact with your body chemistry. The aim is a holistic approach — combining food, movement, sleep, and stress into a thorough management strategy rather than an isolated meal plan.

It's user-friendly, written for everyday life, and easy to start the same week you read it.

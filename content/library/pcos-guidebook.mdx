A comprehensive resource for understanding and managing Polycystic Ovary Syndrome. The PCOS Guidebook takes a holistic approach — diet, lifestyle, mindset — and walks you through each piece in language that respects your time and your intelligence.

Inside you'll find clear explanations of the hormonal imbalances behind PCOS and how they show up in the body — weight, skin, mood, energy, fertility, sleep. Dietary strategies that actually balance hormones and support sustainable weight loss, with meal plans, recipes, and clear guidance on what to eat and what to skip.

The guidebook also covers exercise routines that help manage PCOS symptoms (and the ones that work against you), techniques to develop a positive mindset and reduce stress, practical tips for improving sleep, and a clear-headed look at the supplements with real evidence behind them — plus an overview of available medications, their benefits, and their potential side effects.

This is the book I wished existed when I started practising — a practical, complete companion for the women who are doing this work without enough support.

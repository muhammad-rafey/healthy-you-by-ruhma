Skin Secrets offers straightforward advice on improving skin conditions — acne, eczema, psoriasis, and rosacea — through diet and a clear-headed look at the hormones underneath. It's written for women who've tried the topical-only route and want to understand what their food is doing to their face.

You'll learn how what you eat affects your skin, and which vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants actually support a clear complexion. The guidebook explains how gut health influences your skin and gives practical, evidence-based advice on the dietary changes that help.

There's special attention given to hormonal acne — the kind that arrives on schedule and ignores any cream you put on it. The book helps you understand the connection between hormones and breakouts, and provides practical solutions and lifestyle adjustments to manage and reduce hormonal acne effectively.

Like all the guidebooks in the Library, it's not a substitute for individual medical advice — but it gives you the framework to have a much better conversation with your clinician.

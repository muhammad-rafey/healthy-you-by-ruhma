In my Diet Planning Program your unique dietary requirements are the brief. The goal is a nutritional roadmap that aligns with your current routine — not a copy-paste meal plan that asks you to upend your life.

## What you will experience

- A healthier daily rhythm — meals that work with your schedule, not against it.
- A real shift in energy, the kind you can feel by mid-afternoon.
- Lower stress on your system, with better sleep and easier mornings.
- A new sense of fulfilment around food — the part most diet plans miss.
- Mental clarity, focus, and the cognitive ease that comes from eating well.

## What I offer

- Customised diet plans designed around your specific needs and conditions.
- Home-based workout suggestions for the days you don't want to leave the house.
- 24/7 access to my support — questions, course corrections, the hard days.
- Sustainability strategies so the changes you make this month still work in six.
- Continuous coaching that respects your preferences, restrictions, and life.

## A small gift inside the program

Every Diet Planning client gets a free consultation call to use whenever they like — for the rest of their life. You can transfer it to a friend or family member if you'd prefer.

## Sample week

A real plan from a recent client (names and details changed for privacy). Yours will look different — every plan does.

- **Monday.** Soaked oats with chia, almonds, and seasonal fruit. Lentil dal with brown rice and cucumber raita. An afternoon handful of pistachios. Grilled fish with sautéed greens and a small portion of jeera rice.
- **Tuesday.** Two egg omelette with spinach and feta. Chicken karahi with roti and a side salad. Apple with a tablespoon of peanut butter. Daal chawal with kachumber.
- **Wednesday.** Greek yoghurt with walnuts and honey. Vegetable biryani (controlled portion) with raita. Roasted chickpeas. Seekh kebabs with grilled vegetables and a small naan.
- **Thursday.** Smoothie with banana, oats, milk, and flax. Palak paneer with roti. Carrot sticks with hummus. Grilled chicken with quinoa and roasted vegetables.
- **Friday.** Egg paratha (one) with mint chutney. Mixed daal with rice and salad. A few dates. Whole-fish curry with steamed greens.
- **Saturday.** A small breakfast — your choice. The point of Saturday is to test what you've learned: a wedding, a dinner out, a friend's plate of biryani. We talk about how it went on Sunday.
- **Sunday.** A reset day. Light meals, hydration, a long walk, and notes for the week ahead.

## Frequently asked

- **How will you help me reach my goal?** We set a monthly milestone together. If you put in the work and don't reach it, there's no charge for the next month — your success is our priority.
- **How long until I see results?** Programs are structured around ninety days. The exact arc depends on where you start, what you're solving for, and how consistently the plan is followed.
- **How often does the plan change?** Usually every fifteen days. Some clients need more frequent updates, some less — we tune the cadence to your progress.
- **Can I pay in installments?** Yes — monthly or fortnightly, with no interest or extra fees.
- **Is there a guarantee?** If you don't see changes within forty days, you can stop the program and I'll return half the fee, without questions.
- **What if I need to end the program early?** Life is uncertain. We'll part ways amicably and find an arrangement that's fair to both of us.

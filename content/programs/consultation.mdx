A consultation call is the best place to start when you're not sure what's wrong, what to do, or whether the influencer's advice you saw last week applies to you. It's thirty-five minutes with a real clinician, on a video call, with the time to actually listen.

## When this is the right call

- You're confused about diet questions and not sure who to trust.
- You've been Googling symptoms for weeks and getting contradictory answers.
- You want a single, clear evaluation of your current diet — what's working, what isn't, what to change first.
- You're considering a program but want to talk to me before you commit.

## What you'll get out of it

- Advice that fits your real lifestyle, your goals, and your preferences.
- Professional experience and evidence-based insights — not a script.
- Practical strategies you can implement the same day.
- A way to stop wasting time on online research.
- Resolved doubts about nutrition, weight loss, supplements, or meal planning — in a single session.

## What I promise

- A safe, non-judgemental space to share whatever you came with.
- Clear, concise, actionable advice without jargon.
- Recommendations grounded in evidence — not pushed by product partnerships.
- Inclusive guidance that respects your food preferences and dietary restrictions.
- Attention to the lifestyle factors — stress, sleep, habits — that most short calls skip.

## Free ebooks after the call

After the consultation you'll receive my full ebook library — Diabetes Essentials, the PCOS Guidebook, and Skin Secrets — at no extra charge. The reading isn't a substitute for the call; it's a way to keep going on the questions we surfaced together.

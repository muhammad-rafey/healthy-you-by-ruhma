The Coaching Program is a ninety-day partnership for the whole of your health — hormonal balance, gut health, mental wellbeing, and the confidence that comes from understanding your own body. It isn't a meal plan with a website behind it; it's a coach in your corner for three months, with the structure to make changes stick.

## What we work on together

- **Hormonal health.** PCOS, thyroid, cortisol, fertility — the conditions that hide behind "general fatigue" and "stubborn weight."
- **Confidence.** The kind that comes from sleeping well, eating well, and recognising the person in the mirror.
- **Gut health.** Digestion, bloating, food sensitivities, the microbiome — the foundation everything else sits on.
- **Mental wellbeing.** Stress management, mood, sleep — because the rest of the work doesn't hold without these.

## What's included

- **Customised diet plans** that update as your body responds.
- **Workout plans** built for home — no gym, no equipment that doesn't fit.
- **Sustainability strategies** so the changes you make this month survive the next three.
- **Unlimited support.** Message me when you need to. Mid-flight, mid-wedding, mid-grocery-store.
- **Real-life enjoyment.** Plans for the parties, the trips, the days when "stick to it" isn't going to happen — and that's fine.
- **The full ebook library**, included with the program at no extra cost.

## What you walk away with

- A complete transformation into the version of yourself you came here for.
- The end of needing a coach for the same problem ever again — you'll have the tools.
- Confidence around social gatherings, family meals, and the parts of life that used to derail progress.
- A real understanding of the root causes of what you came in with — and how to prevent them returning.
- A daily routine that fits your life, not somebody else's.

## Pricing

The Coaching Program runs in three-month blocks starting from PKR 30,000. Installments are available — monthly or fortnightly — with no extra charges for paying in stages.

## Frequently asked

- **How will you help me reach my goal?** We agree a monthly milestone. If you put in the work and don't reach it, there's no charge for the next month.
- **How long until I see results?** The program is structured for ninety days. Most clients feel real changes by week three or four.
- **How often does the plan change?** Usually every fifteen days. Some clients need updates more often; some less. We tune the cadence to your progress.
- **Can I pay in installments?** Yes — monthly or fortnightly, with no interest.
- **Is there a guarantee?** If you don't see changes within forty days, you can stop and I'll return half the fee, no questions asked.
- **What if I need to end the program early?** Life is uncertain. We'll find an arrangement that's fair to both of us.
